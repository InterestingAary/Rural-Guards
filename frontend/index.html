Govt Schemes

PM-KISAN

Direct income support for eligible farmer families. Amount is credited in installments to linked bank account after registration and verification.

Official PM-KISAN portal

PMFBY (Crop Insurance)

Protects crops against losses from drought, flood, cyclone, pest attack, and major weather events. Useful for seasonal risk reduction.

Check crop insurance details

Kisan Credit Card

Provides short-term credit for crop cultivation, allied activities, and selected fishery needs with lower interest compared to many informal options.

Learn about KCC

PM Matsya Sampada Yojana

Support for fish seed, pond development, cold chain, and fish value chain activities. Relevant for fishermen and fish farmers expanding income.

Explore fisheries support

Fishermen Welfare & Safety

State fisheries departments provide accident support, safety-net assistance, and seasonal welfare benefits based on local eligibility rules.

Visit Andhra Pradesh Fisheries

For latest eligibility and documents, visit local Rythu Bharosa Kendra, Agriculture Office, Fisheries Office, or official state portals.
